About Manipur State Museum

History

The Manipur State Museum was established in 1969 with the vision to preserve and showcase the rich cultural heritage of Manipur. Located in Imphal, the museum houses an extensive collection of artifacts that represent the state's diverse history, art, and traditions.

Over the decades, the museum has grown to become one of the premier cultural institutions in Northeast India, serving as a repository of Manipur's historical, archaeological, and ethnographic treasures.

Vision

To be a world-class museum that preserves, interprets, and promotes the cultural heritage of Manipur for present and future generations.

Mission

To collect, conserve, research, and exhibit artifacts that reflect the rich cultural diversity of Manipur while fostering education and cultural appreciation.

Objectives

  • Preserve and protect cultural artifacts and heritage items
  • Conduct research on Manipuri history, art, and culture
  • Provide educational resources and programs for students
  • Organize exhibitions and cultural events
  • Promote awareness of Manipur's cultural heritage
